About Our Society

The Riverside Players have been a cornerstone of local amateur theatre for nearly four decades. Our society was founded by a group of passionate theatre enthusiasts who wanted to bring quality dramatic productions to the community while providing a supportive environment for performers of all experience levels.

We pride ourselves on our inclusive approach to theatre, welcoming members from all walks of life. Whether you're interested in acting, directing, stage management, set design, or any other aspect of theatre production, you'll find a home with us.
