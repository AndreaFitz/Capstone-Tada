About TADA!

The Amateur Dramatic Almanack - Your Ultimate Theatre Resource

Our Mission

TADA! is dedicated to supporting and celebrating amateur theatre throughout the community. We provide resources, connections, and inspiration for theatre enthusiasts at every level.

What We Offer

  • Comprehensive directory of local theatre groups
  • Production announcements and reviews
  • Acting and directing resources
  • Community networking opportunities
  • Educational workshops and events

Join Our Community

Whether you're a seasoned performer or just starting your theatre journey, TADA! welcomes you. Connect with like-minded individuals, discover new opportunities, and be part of the vibrant amateur theatre scene.
