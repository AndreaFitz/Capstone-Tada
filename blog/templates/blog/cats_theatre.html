About C.A.T.S.

Welcome to the Clitheroe Amateur Theatrical Society - where classical theatre lives and breathes!

Founded in 1962, C.A.T.S. has been dedicated to preserving and performing the greatest works of dramatic literature. From Shakespeare's timeless tragedies to Chekhov's profound comedies, we bring the classics to life with passion and authenticity. But we also do Panto now and again - everyone needs a bit of a laugh!

Our Heritage
  • 60+ years of classical theatre
  • Award-winning Shakespeare productions
  • Traditional staging techniques
  • Professional-standard performances
What We Do
  • Classical drama productions
  • Script reading sessions
  • Acting masterclasses
  • Voice and diction training
